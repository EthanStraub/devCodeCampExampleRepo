Features

Pico-8, like other Fantasy Consoles, comes with a variety of tools.

Pico-8 can be played with as if it were like any modern gaming console, only able to run games that other developers publish. The real fun with Fantasy Consoles, however, is tinkering with what lies under the hood.

Some features of Pico-8 include:

  • A code editor operating only with capital letters and limited built-in functions.
  • A sprite editor comprising of just 16 colors.
  • A map editor that uses said sprites in 256x256 tile array.
  • A sound effect and music maker.
  • The option to export your game's "Cartridge" to the png format.
  • The option to publish your game's "Cartridge" on the lexaloffle Pico-8 forums,
    where other developers can comment on your work and play it for themselves.
