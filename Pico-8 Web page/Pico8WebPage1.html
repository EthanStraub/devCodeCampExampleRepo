Introduction

Pico-8 is what is referred to as a "Fantasy Console".

"Fantasy Consoles" are computer programs meant to emulate older PC gaming systems such as the Commodore-64, in the sense that they are intentionally limited in quality such as graphics and sound in order to achieve a retro feel. Similarly, the developer tools that come with Fantasy Consoles are also limited, with the restrictions of Pico-8 being a 65536 character limit, as well as a 8192 "token" limit. These limitations are put in place in order to force game developers to push the software to its absolute limits while still making a quality product, a challenge in and of itself completely outside regular game development.
